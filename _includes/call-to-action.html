About Me!

When I was growing up, I enjoyed the fact that I could be inspired by so many different types of people; philosophers, artists, lawyers, sportsmen, scientists etc. I did not like the idea that after leaving University I would have to pigeonhole myself into a certain bracket. Ie. “I am Jake, the banker” or “Have you met Jake?, He is a lawyer”.
I want to be able to do lots of different things and have a more rounded existence. I enjoy and extract value from the cross-fertilization of disciplines. It is therefore my intention to have the freedom of being pan-sector. One of the really cool things about tech is that it allows you to do that.
I intend to engage and be a part of lots of different weird and wonderful things. So long as it interests me and I can learn something from it then I am game. The way that I will go about doing this is by helping entrepreneurs go from ideation to product.
